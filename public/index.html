üîç Brief About the Prototype

LegalAI Demystifier is a comprehensive AI-powered platform that transforms complex legal documents into clear, actionable insights specifically designed for the Indian legal ecosystem. Built entirely on Google Cloud infrastructure, it employs a sophisticated multi-agent AI system to analyze legal documents, assess risks, and provide constitutional compliance guidance in multiple Indian languages.

üéØ Problem Addressed

Legal documents in India are filled with complex jargon, creating information asymmetry that exposes citizens to financial and legal risks. Our platform bridges this gap by making legal information accessible to everyone.

üöÄ Our Solution

A multi-agent AI system that provides plain-language summaries, risk assessments, constitutional compliance checks, and fraud detection - all grounded in Indian law and available in multiple languages.
